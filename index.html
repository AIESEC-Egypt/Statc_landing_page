What's AIESEC?

AIESEC is the largest non for profit youth organisation in the world, being present in more than 120 countries we provide experiences for young people to develop their leadership skills as well as boost their professional skills.

AIESEC aims for Peace & Fulfillment of the Humankind's Potential. Its members are interested in world issues, leadership and management. AIESEC does not discriminate on the basis of ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion or national/social origin. AIESEC is non-political, independent organisation run by youth for youth

AIESEC

AIESEC is a non-governmental not-for-profit organisation in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), affiliated with the UN DPI, member of ICMYO, and is recognized by UNESCO. AIESEC International is registered as a Foundation (Stichting), RSIN #807103895 in Rotterdam, The Netherlands.
