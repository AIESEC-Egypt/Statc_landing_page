Program Overview

Global Talent Program provides leadership experiences to young people in a global learning environment through professional internships abroad in major upcoming sectors, like the: Educational Sector. Marketing and Business Sector, and IT.
These internships present an opportunity to improve your skills and enhance your knowledge in a domain of your choice, while building your personal and professional network of people from different countries and cultures.This multi-cultural exposure will broaden your perspective and power drive your career graph as international work experience in the globalization context is considered immensely valuable.
